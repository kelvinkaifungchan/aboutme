AAxAmnesty International Pavillion (Studio*) London, UK Competition 2019
Efforts need to be made in understanding the material ecology of mycelium and timber specific to the conditions of Syria. The proposal extends the brief to reconsider the approach to construction in conflict zones, and the potential of mycelium as an insulation material as opposed to form generator.
ArupxCricursa Glasstec Exhibition (Studio*) Dusseldorf, Germany Competition 2019
PARADISE (Ancient Persian: Pairi - Daēza = Walled Enclosure): The idea of a garden is a paradise that we can control - equally part of yet separate to the world. The proposal is a series of standard and bespoke Cricursa parts assembled to create a continuous wall, defining the perimeter of a garden. Spaces are activated by Glasstec delegates who can stop to sit, socialise and admire the possibilities of Cricursa’s technology.
The Switch Seoul, South Korea Research 2019
The Switch proposes an additive system to the existing food distribution ecology in Seoul, South Korea, to reconnect the suburb of Gasan-Dong. The system is imagined as four layers of supports: Network, Infrastructure, Enclosure, Furniture allowing a constant renegotiation of the architecture over time.
